About Page

Bonchon means "my hometown" in Korean. Our story starts in Busan, South Korea. That’s where our founder, Jinduk Seo, perfected the recipe for Bonchon’s signature fried chicken and sauces. He then traveled to the United States in 2006 to share his mouthwatering Korean comfort food with the world.

BonChon is a global restaurant chain known for its signature Korean double-fried chicken and Asian fusion cuisine, with a strong focus on sharing the joy of Korean comfort food worldwide. The brand originated in Busan, South Korea, and has since expanded to numerous countries. BonChon's mission is to share the joy of Korean comfort food around the world.

Founded by Jinduk Seo, Bonchon expanded from one small restaurant to a global phenomenon, staying true to its Korean roots while inspiring local communities with every meal.

Jinduk Seo remains a passionate advocate of authentic Korean cuisine, ensuring Bonchon’s standards are kept high wherever it opens its doors.
